## Design Context

### Users
Developers, indie hackers, and hiring reviewers exploring a file-first S3 storage dashboard. They use the product to browse, upload, and manage files across a managed drive and connected object storage.

### Brand Personality
Sharp, calm, technical. The interface should feel competent, fast, and serious rather than playful or decorative.

### Aesthetic Direction
Vercel-like restraint with shadcn-style primitives. Flat layout, sidebar navigation, minimal surfaces, low visual noise, and no "cards on a board" composition. Prefer clean borders, spacing, and hierarchy over decorative chrome.

### Design Principles
1. Keep surfaces flat and structural, not floating.
2. Use hierarchy through spacing, typography, and borders instead of heavy containers.
3. Favor a serious developer-tool feel over dashboard candy.
4. Keep interactions obvious and fast, with very little ornament.
5. Use shadcn-compatible patterns while staying visually restrained.
